The rule says that for every 20 minutes spent looking at a screen,
a person should look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds
Following the rule is a great way to remember to take frequent breaks.
This should reduce eye strain caused by looking at digital screens for too long.
This is free software which turns your display window off every 20 minutes.
